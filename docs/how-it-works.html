How it works

Memryo is designed to be simple and fast.

Quick steps

  1. 1
    Make it yours – Use templates or create your own categories and activities. Name them, set how often they repeat (daily to yearly), and organize everything your way.
  2. 2
    Log with one tap – Open the app, select an activity, and tap "Remember it." The current date and time are recorded instantly.
  3. 3
    View your timeline – See all events in a scrollable timeline. Filter by activity to answer "When did I last…?"
  4. 4
    Add notes when needed – Tap any event to add or edit notes (e.g. mileage, filter size). Edit or delete events anytime.

Detailed description

Memryo helps you never forget the past. It's a simple, private activity tracker for recurring tasks, maintenance, and habits—with one-tap logging and a clear timeline so you always know when you last did something.

Smart activity management

Choose from common activities or create your own. Set how often each one repeats (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly) and organize everything by category.

One-tap "Remember it" button

Log the current date and time in one tap. Perfect when you're busy and just want to record that you did it.

Timeline history

View all your events in a simple timeline. Filter by activity, see patterns, and never wonder "when did I last…?" again. See a sample timeline.

Notes on events

Add notes to any event (e.g. "Oil changed at 45,000 miles" or "Filter size: 20x25"). Edit or delete events anytime.

100% customizable — make it truly yours

Your categories, your activity names, your recurrence (hourly to yearly). Start with built-in templates or create everything from scratch. Memryo doesn't impose a structure; you design the app around your life.

Privacy-first

All data is stored only on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store your information on our servers. No account or sign-in required.

Who it's for

Anyone who forgets when they last did routine tasks; people tracking home or car maintenance; anyone building habits or tracking recurring health tasks; small teams or individuals who want timestamped records without spreadsheets.

Memryo is free to use. Your data stays on your phone—private and under your control.
