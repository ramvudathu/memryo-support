Memryo – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Memryo ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the Memryo mobile application.

1. Data storage

All data you create in Memryo (activities, events, notes, and categories) is stored only on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store this data on our servers or any third-party servers.

2. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, and the app is not designed to collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device identifiers for advertising or tracking
  • Location data
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. Children’s privacy

Memryo does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we do not collect personal information, the app can be used by users of all ages without collection of personal data.

4. Third parties

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social networks. Your data is not shared with third parties.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Memryo’s practices, you can contact us at memryoapp@gmail.com.
