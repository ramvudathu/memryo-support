Memryo – Support

Thank you for using Memryo. We're here to help.

Getting started

Memryo helps you track your activities and events. All your data is stored locally on your device for privacy.

Features

  • Select categories and activities
  • Record events with date and time (one-tap “Remember it”)
  • View event history in a timeline
  • Add notes to events
  • Manage categories and activities

Privacy

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information. No data is sent to external servers.

Frequently asked questions

How do I add a new activity?

Tap “Add New Activity,” then enter the activity name and select a category.

How do I record an event?

Select an activity, then tap the “Remember it” button to record the current date and time.

Can I edit or delete events?

Yes. Tap an event in the history to edit the date, time, or notes. You can also delete events.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. It's never transmitted to external servers.
